The project is about cigarettes and how they impact the environment in a harmful way. Cigarettes release toxic fumes that are made of chemicals such as carbon monoxide, arsenic, ammonia, acetone, toluene, methylamine. The solution is to create two prototypes that can filter the air so that we can disperse it back into the environment. The prototypes will be eco-friendly meaning that we will be using clean sources of energy. Our main source of energy will be solar energy because it provides enough energy for the machine.
